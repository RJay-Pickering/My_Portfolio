About Me

Welcome to my website! I am a graduate at South Pontotoc High School and currently a student at Base Camp Coding Academy in Water Valley, MS. I also work at my farmland, Pickering Hereford Hogs, where we sell Hereford Hogs and tend to the other animals like the cows and the dogs. My hobbies include playing video games, working on machines (like trucks, tractors, electric tools, etc.), and writing short stories. When I started at Base Camp, I had no coding experience, but I was determined to learn. What got me determined to learn coding is my PlayStation. When I was eight, I got my first console and I always played on it as a way to relax and have fun. I have made so many great memories from that game, and I would like to make games that others would enjoy as well.
