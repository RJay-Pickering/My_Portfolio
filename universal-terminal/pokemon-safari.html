Pokémon Safari

A classmate and I worked together for a unit project to build something in Python. We decided to make a Pokémon safari park game revolving around the third generation Pokémon's. The player can have random encounters with Pokémon in the wild or they can go fight the gym leaders to obtain a Bronze, Silver, and Gold Medal. We also added a Legendary Pokémon that you can find and catch after obtaining the Golden Medal from the battle towers. From this project, I learned teamwork and worked on my communication skills. In this demo, if you beat the gold tower, it will not save your name in the hall of champions.
