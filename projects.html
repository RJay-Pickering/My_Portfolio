We discussed what we could do for our unit project and decided to make a Pokemon safari park game revolving around the third generation Pokemon's. You go and have random encounters with pokemon in the wild or you can go fight the gym leaders to obtain a Bronze, Silver, and Gold Metal. We also added a legendary that you can find and catch after obtaining the Golden Metal from the battle towers.
